Jooi Network - Next Generation of Cryptocurrency

Greetings! We present you Jooi Network, a high-tech project based on decentralized cryptocurrency trading.

For several years our team of experienced financiers, cryptocurrency traders, and analysts has been actively working on creating and improving a new cryptocurrency, token on the Solana platform.

Now our goal is to create a decentralized online exchange for trading and develop a mobile application for it, which will allow the user to manage their assets conveniently and quickly.

The comfort of investors and users has always been and always will be in the first place for our team, so we put most of our resources and efforts into the creation of a fast and reliable technical core as well as a convenient and intuitive interface.

At the moment, we are finalizing the creation of our online exchange, including the thorough testing of its reliability and performance. At the same time, we are finalizing the preparation of the Jooi Network token for the ICO stage.
